Green Logistics

Green Logistics Is a delivery company that believes in protecting the environment. while satisfying customers with fast and secure delivery. They archive their goal by using E-vehicles for operations and recyclable materials for packaging.

For simplicity and easy recognition the logo mark was designed with the initials G and L alongside a rectangular version of a recycle icon
