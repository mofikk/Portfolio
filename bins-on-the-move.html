Design Brief

Kourtney wanted a redesign for her moving bin rental business. She aims to replace traditional cardboard boxes with rented bins. Kourtney requires two logo variations: one without art for application on the rental bins themselves and one with art for digital and print collateral such as social media and business cards.

Design Preferences:

Style: Clean, modern, and engaging. Avoid overly professional or bland designs.
Typography: A fun font preferred, but not whimsical. It should exude professionalism while retaining a sense of approachability.
Layout: Preferably square layout over round.
Color Scheme: Vibrant green is desired to contrast against the black bins and to add a pop of color.
